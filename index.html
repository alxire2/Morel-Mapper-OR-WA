Picking Tips

Elevation: 2,500-5,500 ft. Season moves uphill March-July.
Trees: Mixed conifer: Douglas fir, grand fir, ponderosa, true firs.
Slope: S-facing first, then N-facing as season progresses.
Severity: Moderate burn > scorched earth. Near partially burned conifers.
Permits: USFS free personal use permit required on NF land.
